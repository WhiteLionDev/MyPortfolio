Resumé

I'm an independent video game developer, currently based in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

I graduated from Universidad Tecnologica Nacional of Argentina, with Associate Degree in Computer Programming. During school, I got fascinated by discovering the existence of RPG Maker, with it, I gave my firsts steps into video game development. At that time, I did nothing worth to mention, but that first experience work for me to know that I can make games and that I must make games.

During and following college, I always kept learning and working to make my mark into this world. I joined INVI Games as Game Programmer after finishing my studies.

As a core member of INVI Games I had the chance to teamwork in an exciting project from sketch idea to Steam publication.

After the release of Drop Alive, I have been working independently with collaborators. I had a great experience making educational video games from which both The Stolen Jewel and Decimal Dungeon emerged.

So, if you had read this far, I want to hear from you! I'm always on the lookout for new interesting projects to work on, so if you think you have one, send me an email!
